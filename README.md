# BuildsUp

A construction marketplace platform available on web and mobile. Connects buyers and sellers of construction materials, chemicals, services, and labour across availability and requirement listings.

## What it does

- **Materials & Chemicals** — List and discover construction materials and chemical products for sale or requirement
- **Services** — Post and find construction-related services
- **Labour** — Connect contractors and workers based on availability and job requirements
- **Marketplace** — Browse, search, and manage listings across all categories

## Platforms

- Web app
- Mobile app (iOS & Android)
